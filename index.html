so... where are you? what is this? and who am I

I’m Renee. I'm a creator at heart and in all facets of my life.
At work this looks like building more efficient systems and processes for our internal team.
But in my own time I like to get my hands dirty, painting, making pottery and most recently making jewellery.

This - Unearthly Entity is an effort to be more authentic and vulnerable - intentionally taking myself out of my comfort zone and sharing this part of myself with you.
(and to hold myself accountable to continue doing this)And I'm hopelessly hopeful that we'll be able to make the world a slightly more homely place.‍

I'm a person, living in Melbourne & passionate about learning, living and working towards a slightly better world..
